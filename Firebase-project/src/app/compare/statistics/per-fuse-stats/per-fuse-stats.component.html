Total usage per circuit
This chart shows the total amount of energy in kWh used for each circuit. You can edit the timeframe via the button in the top right.
A Watt hour (Wh) is a unit of energy, which tells us about the amount of work that has been done by some devices. A kWh or kilo Watt hour is just 1000 Watt hours. Watt hours refer to enegy used / work done for some time period. For example: a 60 Watt lightbulb that is on for 1 hour will consume 1 Wh of energy.
