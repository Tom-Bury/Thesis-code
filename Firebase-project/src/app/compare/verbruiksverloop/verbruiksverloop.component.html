Total usage distribution
This chart shows how the total amount of Watts usage varies in time for the selected timeframe. You can edit the timeframe via the button in the top right. A Watt is a unit of power, which means the rate at which energy is consumed/produced. For example: a device of 100 Watt will consume energy faster than a 50 Watt device. You can interpret the area under this curve as the actual amount of consumed energy.
